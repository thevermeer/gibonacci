Generalized Fibonacci Sequences and Lucas Numbers

The General form of Fibonacci sequences takes the form of a series of sequence of numbers.
Each number is given by f(p, n), where p is the number of previous terms added together to the nth number in a sequence.
For instance, the fibonacci sequence would be expressed as f(2, n)
